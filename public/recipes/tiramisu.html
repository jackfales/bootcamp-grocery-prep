Tiramisu

The best dessert ever.


Serves 1

Ingredients

  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 0.5 cup(s) granulated sugar divided
  • 0.75 cup(s) heavy cream
  • 1 cup(s) mascarpone
  • 1.75 cups good espresso or very strong coffee
  • 2 tablespoons rum or cognac
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 24 ladyfingers

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, whip together the egg yolks and half the sugar. Transfer mixture to a large bowl and set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl, whip cream and other half of sugar until it creates soft-medium peaks. Add mascarpone and continue to whip.
  3. Gently fold the mascarpone mixture into the sweetened egg yolks until combined.
  4. Combine espresso and rum in a shallow bowl and set aside.
  5. Using a sifter, dust the bottom of a 2-quart baking dish with half the cocoa powder.
  6. Dip each ladyfinger into the espresso mixture and spread across the pan.
  7. Spread half the mascarpone mixture onto the landyfingers into one even layer.
  8. Repeat with remaining ladyfingers.
  9. Dust top layer with remaining cocoa powder.
  10. Cover with plastic wrap and let it chill in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours.
