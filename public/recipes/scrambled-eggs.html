Scrambled Eggs

Who doesn't love some good ole scrambled eggs?


Serves 1

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tbsp whole milk or water
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Place a skillet on the stovetop on medium low heat.
  2. Meanwhile, break eggs into a bowl with a tablespoon of water or milk, and some salt and pepper.
  3. Whisk to beat the eggs briskly for 30-60 seconds until mixed well.
  4. Lightly grease your hot skillet with a thin layer of oil or butter.
  5. Pour the eggs into the skillet. Use a sptula to pull the cooked outer edges in towards the center of the eggs.
  6. Repeat until the eggs are soft and fluffy.
