Egg Sandwich

This time put it in a sandwich


Serves 1

Ingredients

  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 1 egg(s)
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Place a skillet on the stovetop on medium low heat.
  2. Melt the unsalted butter in the skiller
  3. Break egg(s) into the skillet
  4. Lightly season with salt and pepper
  5. Toast the sourdough bread
  6. Once cooked, put the egg inbetween the slices of bread
